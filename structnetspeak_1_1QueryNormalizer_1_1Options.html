uint32_t netspeak::QueryNormalizer::Options::max_length

The maximum length norm queries are allowed to have.

This means that only phrases up to this length can be matched by the norm queries.

size_t netspeak::QueryNormalizer::Options::max_regex_matches

The maximum number of words each regex can be replaced with.

A regex will (usually) be replaced with an alternation of words that match that regex. This value determines with how many words each regex will be replaced with at most.
