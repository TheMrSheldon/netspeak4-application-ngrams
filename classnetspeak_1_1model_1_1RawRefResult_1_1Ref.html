Frequency netspeak::model::RawRefResult::Ref::freq ( ) const
inline

A bounded frequency of the referenced phrase.

This frequency is bounded by the underlying integer type and may not be the exact frequency. Only very few phrases (most only stop words) will have frequencies great enough to be affect by this.
